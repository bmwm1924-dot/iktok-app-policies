Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("the App") handles information. The App is a personal automation tool used by its developer to post video content to a single, self-owned TikTok account.

1. Information We Access

The App accesses TikTok account credentials (via TikTok's official OAuth process) and video content solely to upload posts to the developer's own TikTok account. No data is collected from, or shared with, any third party.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process personal data belonging to other TikTok users, viewers, or any third party.

3. Data Storage

Any access tokens or credentials used by the App are stored only as needed to maintain the connection to the developer's own TikTok account and are not shared with or sold to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

No data accessed by the App is shared with third parties, except as required to communicate with TikTok's own API in order to publish content.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Credentials and tokens are retained only as long as needed for the App to function and can be revoked at any time via TikTok's account settings. Users may request deletion of any retained data by contacting the developer.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under the age required to hold a TikTok account.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
